Terms and Conditions


1. The policy will come into effect after a period of 6 months.

2. The maximum age for family coverage is 50 years,
but all the primary members must be 21 years or older.
Children under the age of 18 must be included under the main member's coverage.

3. Options related to family cover allow for extended family members to enjoy cover.
Extended family includes but is not limited to: Aunt, Uncle, Sister, etc.
Every individual will be subjected to the pricing schedule.


- Age 0-50 : R120
- Age 51+ : R170

4. Premiums must be paid by the 15th of evey month.

5. the waiting period will not appy for members in the event of an accidental death.

6. if the payment of the cover is behind by 60 days, the policy will lapse and all paid premiums will be forfeited

7. if a member choose to upgrade or downgrade their coveragee, waiting period shall again apply concerning the new benefits, from the date of the upgrade/ downgrade.
